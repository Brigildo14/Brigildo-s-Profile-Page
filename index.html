Brigildo_14 is a Brazilian e-professional footballer who plays for P1R4TES FA and the EFA Angola national team. He most notably played as a centre-back for P1RATES FA, whom he captained for 3 seasons. A versatile player who has previously played as a striker and midfielder in his career. Known for his goalscoring capabilities, leadership skills, reckless and aggressive defending, he is considered one of the greatest defenders of his generation.

After emerging through Nukenis FC and spending one season with them, Brigildo_14 moved to P1R4TES FA. Since then, he went on to become a mainstay for P1R4TES FA, reaching the Premier League third place in season #3 & #5, and the League Cup third place in the season #3 (both competions organised by EFA Angola).
